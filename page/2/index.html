About Me

Written on October 11, 2024, in Dublin, Ireland.

Self Introduction

Hello everyone, my name is Li Nansen, previously known as Li Nan. I’m from China and a technology-loving engineer. I hold a bachelor’s degree in Mechatronic Engineering and two master’s degrees in Computer Science: Embedded Systems and Natural Language Processing. I previously worked as a Go Language Engineer at a major internet company in China, and I am currently pursuing a master’s degree at a university in Dublin.

I am deeply interested in frontend and backend development, system architecture, algorithms, game development, and generative AI (e.g., Stable Diffusion and ChatGPT). I’ve always been dedicated to improving my programming skills and enjoy experimenting with new technologies, keeping myself sensitive to industry trends.

If you share similar interests in technology or lifestyle, feel free to reach out to me. We could discuss and exchange ideas together.

Ramblings

I know you might be curious about me, so I’ll let ChatGPT come up with some questions, and I’ll answer them. Let’s see if these questions satisfy your curiosity.

What is your favorite programming language? Why?

I like Go. It’s simple, modern, and efficient. Its design philosophy is “less is more,” and it embraces minimalism. I appreciate languages with their own unique attitude.

During my student years, I probably enjoyed playing games the most. I’ve loved playing various games since childhood, especially single-player PC games. However, after starting work, I haven’t had the time to continue these hobbies.

What is your favorite movie or TV show? Why?

I rarely use the word “favorite.” I think that most works, if properly appreciated, have their shining points. But the movie that resonates with me the most is “Blade Runner 2049.” The ending, where K lies on the snowy steps and slowly closes his eyes, is filled with loneliness and reflection, which touched me deeply.

As an engineer, what do you think is the biggest challenge?

I think the biggest challenge is people—how to deal with oneself, and how to deal with others. Engineering and science always have solutions, but there is no perfect answer when it comes to people. Fortunately, I always manage to handle this challenge well.

Why did you choose to study in Ireland? How has this experience impacted you?

Initially, my target was the United States, but I later changed my mind and started aiming for other countries, especially English-speaking ones like Australia, Canada, and New Zealand. Ireland has many tech companies and is close to the entire EU market, so I ultimately chose Ireland.

I’m not sure of the specific impact yet, but it’s significant. This experience has profoundly changed my life.

How do you define success?

Knowing what you want, striving for it, and finally achieving it—that is success. I’ve always been searching for what I truly want, but sometimes it feels unclear. Although I’ve achieved many goals, I’m still exploring. Perhaps in others’ eyes, I’m successful, but I think there’s still room for improvement.

Do you enjoy traveling? If you could travel anywhere, where would you go?

Yes, I enjoy traveling. I’ve been to places like the United States, Japan, Australia, and New Zealand. The scenery there is quite beautiful.

If I could, I would go to the Moon. Maybe I’d watch the beautiful Earth from there. Perhaps one day, when I let go of all my attachments, I’ll take off my oxygen mask while gazing at the Earth. But, of course, I think that day will never come!

关于我

本文书写于 2024年10月11日，都柏林，爱尔兰。

自我介绍

大家好，我叫李楠森，曾用名李楠。我来自中国，是一名热爱科技的工程师。我拥有机械电子的本科学位和两个计算机科学的硕士学位，分别是嵌入式系统和自然语言处理。我曾在中国一家大型互联网公司担任过 Go 语言工程师，目前正在都柏林一所大学攻读硕士学位。

我对前端和后端开发、系统架构、算法、游戏开发，以及生成式 AI（例如 Stable Diffusion 和 ChatGPT）有着浓厚的兴趣。我一直致力于提高自己的编程能力，也喜欢尝试新技术，保持对行业发展的敏感度。

如果你对技术或者生活方式有相同的兴趣，欢迎联系我，我们可以一起讨论和交流。

碎碎念

我知道你会对我本人好奇，我会让 ChatGPT 提出一些问题，然后我再回答。看看这些问题是否满足了你的好奇心。

你最喜欢的编程语言是什么？为什么？

我喜欢 Go 语言。它简洁、现代且高效。它的设计理念是“少即是多”，非常节制，我喜欢有自己态度的语言。
该做什么。学生时代可能最喜欢玩游戏吧，从小就喜欢玩各种游戏，特别是 PC 端的单机游戏。不过，工作以后这些爱好也没有时间再继续下去了。

你最喜欢的电影或电视剧是哪部？为什么？

我很少会说“喜欢”这个词，其实大部分作品如果认真欣赏，都会有闪光点。不过，最让我难以释怀的电影应该是《银翼杀手 2049》。结局中，K 躺在雪中的台阶上，慢慢合上了双眼，那一刻充满了孤独与反思，让我深有共鸣。

作为一个工程师，你认为最大的挑战是什么？

我觉得最大的挑战是人——如何处理自己，如何处理他人。工程和科学总有解决办法，但人没有一个真正的最优解。而幸运的是，我总能处理好这个挑战。

你为什么选择留学爱尔兰？这段经历对你有什么影响？

最初，我的目标是美国，但是后来我改变了主意，我开始瞄准其他国家，特别是英语国家，如澳大利亚、加拿大、新西兰。爱尔兰有很多科技公司，紧邻整个欧盟市场，所以我最终选择了爱尔兰。

我不知道这段经历的具体影响，但它非常重大。这次经历彻底改变了我。

在你看来，成功的定义是什么？

知道自己想要什么，并为之努力，最终实现——这就是成功。我一直在寻找自己真正想要的东西，但有时候觉得目标并不明确，虽然也实现了很多目标，但还在不断探索中。也许在别人眼中我是成功的，但我觉得还有很多改进的地方。

你喜欢旅行吗？如果可以去任何地方旅行，你会选择哪里？

喜欢，我曾去过美国，日本，澳大利亚，新西兰等地方旅行。那里的景色都不错。

如果可以，我会去月球。我可能会在月球上看着美丽的地球。也许有一天，当我放下了一切执念，我会在那时摘下自己的氧气面罩，当然，我想这一天永远不会到来！
